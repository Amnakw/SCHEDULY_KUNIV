Not yet registered?

If you are a student or an instructor in the Computer Science department in Kuwait University you must first email us at askscheduly@gmail.com or contact a staff memeber to make your account.


If you're a student


After you login please access the progress form and then then fill it the courses you have passed.

*Please do not set courses you are currently taking as completed.

To view the suggested plan go to plan page.

The plan begins from the current semester onwards.
The number of credit in the rows after the semester represenet credits you need take in:
  • Computer Science department elective courses. (You can find them in the wish list page)
  • Collage of Scinece courses For more information please visit the course cataloge
  • General University electives For more information please visit the course cataloge
You can register a wish list of elective Computer Science courses during the time period allowed.
To know the time period for wish list period, remember to check the emails and we will let you know.

To change your email or password please visit the profile page
Remember the email must be active to recieve wish list times.
If you lose the password to your account please contact askscheduly@gmail.com
